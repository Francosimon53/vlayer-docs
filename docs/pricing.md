# Pricing

vlayer offers three tiers to fit your team's needs, from individual developers to enterprise healthcare organizations.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I try Pro before paying?

Yes! Pro includes a **14-day free trial** with no credit card required. Start your trial at [app.vlayer.app/pricing](https://app.vlayer.app/pricing).

### What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) via Stripe. Enterprise customers can also pay by invoice (NET 30).

### Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your Pro subscription at any time. You'll retain access until the end of your billing period.

### Do you offer discounts for non-profits or educational institutions?

Yes! Contact us at [hello@vlayer.app](mailto:hello@vlayer.app) for non-profit and academic pricing.

### What's included in the free trial?

The 14-day free trial includes full access to all Pro features: dashboard, templates, team management, PDF reports, and email support.

### How does team pricing work?

Pro includes up to 10 team members. Enterprise plans support unlimited team members with no per-seat fees.

### Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes, you can upgrade from Open Source to Pro instantly. Downgrading from Pro to Open Source will take effect at the end of your billing period. Contact sales for Enterprise plan changes.

### What happens to my data if I cancel?

Your scan data remains accessible for 30 days after cancellation. After that, it's permanently deleted. You can export your data anytime before deletion.

### Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for annual Pro subscriptions. Monthly subscriptions are non-refundable but can be cancelled anytime.
